Rules and History of the Czech Card Game Prší

Prší is a traditional Czech card game played with a 32-card deck. The game involves players trying to get rid of all their cards by matching the suit or rank of the top card on the discard pile. Special cards allow players to skip opponents, reverse the play order, or force opponents to draw extra cards.

This project is a simple card game created as a showcase for an asset pack designed by students from an art school. It serves as a presentation of their work and as inspiration for how graphic assets can be used in a small game.
